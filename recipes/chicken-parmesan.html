Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is a classic Italian-American dish that consists of breaded chicken breast, topped with tomato sauce and melted cheese. The dish is believed to have originated in the United States, where Italian immigrants adapted traditional Italian recipes to suit local tastes and ingredients. Today, Chicken Parmesan is a popular and beloved comfort food in many parts of the world.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Oil for frying
  • Marinara sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Cooked pasta (optional)
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice chicken breasts in half horizontally to make thinner pieces.
  3. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  4. In a shallow dish, beat 2 eggs. In another dish, combine breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  5. Dip chicken in egg mixture, then coat with breadcrumb mixture.
  6. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook until golden brown, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  7. Transfer chicken to a baking dish, top with marinara sauce and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake for 20-25 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Serve chicken Parmesan with cooked pasta and garnish with fresh basil.
