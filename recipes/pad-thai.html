Pad Thai

Description

Pad Thai is a popular Thai dish, characterized by its rice noodles, tofu, shrimp, and peanuts, all tossed in a tangy sauce. It's a flavorful and satisfying meal that can be easily customized with various proteins and vegetables to suit individual tastes. Originating from Thailand, Pad Thai has gained worldwide popularity and is now a staple in Thai cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Tofu
  • Shrimp
  • Bean sprouts
  • Scallions
  • Egg
  • Ground peanuts
  • Tamarind paste
  • Fish sauce
  • Palm sugar
  • Garlic
  • Chili powder
  • Lime
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Soak rice noodles in warm water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a small bowl, mix tamarind paste, fish sauce, and palm sugar to create the sauce.
  3. In a wok or large skillet, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add garlic and chili powder, then stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  4. Add tofu and shrimp, stir-frying for 2-3 minutes until cooked.
  5. Push ingredients to one side of the wok and crack an egg into the empty space. Scramble until cooked, then mix with the other ingredients.
  6. Add drained rice noodles and sauce, stir-frying for another 2-3 minutes.
  7. Add bean sprouts and scallions, stir-frying for 1 minute.
  8. Transfer to a serving plate, garnish with ground peanuts and lime wedges.
  9. Serve and enjoy your Pad Thai!
